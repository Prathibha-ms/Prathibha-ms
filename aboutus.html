ABOUT CLEAR LAKE

CLEAR LAKE NURSING HOME & DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE has been setting standards since its inception in and regarded as one of the best hospitals out of Kolkata, West Bengal. CLEAR LAKE NURSING HOME & DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE is a 500 bedded multi-speciality nursing home & integrated medical centre based out of Kolkata serving the communities across Howrah and Hooghly district. It is a multi-speciality nursing home with experience in quality healthcare practices that integrate clinical and health care services through constant innovation and education.
